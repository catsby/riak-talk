### What Riak Is 

## Design

- Key/Value data store
- Like Amazon's Dynamo
- Distributed (no master node)
- **AP** of C**AP** Theorem
- Automagically replicating/redistributing (no sharding)
- Schemaless and data-type agnostic
- Written (primarily) in Erlang
